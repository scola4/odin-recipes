Brownie Recipe

Steps

  1. Make sure you have your ingredients
    • Brownie Mix
    • Eggs
    • Butter
    • Water
  2. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees farenheit
  3. Mix the brownie mixture, eggs, and water in a bowl until it is nice and smooth
  4. Pour the batter into a pan
  5. Place the pan in the oven for 25 minutes
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool for 10 minutes
  7. Enjoy!!!

This is a great dessert to go with the chicken parmesan recipe
